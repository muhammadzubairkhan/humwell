Mobile App

Humwell services are offered via its mobile app named Humwell. The app is currently available for the android platform. It provides the following services to its users:

1. Healthcare information - Detailed, searchable information on Hospitals, Clinics, Doctors, Pharmacies, Laboratories and Blood Banks.

2. Management of personal health related records such as prescriptions, lab results and medical purchase receipts.

3. Access to additional Humwell services offered directly by the company or via trusted third party providers.

Medical Professionals & Facilities

Our extensive and ever increasing database of trusted medical professionals, facilities and partner network can be searched and utilized to cater to emergent or planned requirements. Users can rate medical professionals and facilities based on their experience after using their services. Partner professionals can directly book appointments requested by the users and the system will remind about both the patient and physician about the upcoming appointments to make it more likely to happen.

Medical Records

Using the app, the user can get rid of their paper trail of medical files and carry all their previous records with them digitally in their phones. Prescriptions, Reports.

Personal Profiles

Users can manage their own profiles or manage profiles for their loved ones after proper authorization. Health profiles can be built over time to reflect true medical conditions.

Home Medical Delivery

Humwell own medicine delivery service (fulfilled by our partners MyPharmacy) allows the users to order medicines from within the app by just snapping a picture prescription.

MyHealth Diagnostics

Humwell helps its users get constant stream of their critical health data thru its partner. MyHealth PoC (Point of Care) range of devices such as GlucoMeters, BP Monitors.

Hospitals

Commercial hospitals can benefit from the Humwell systems by way of exactly and prominently describing their offered services and facility information to the large number of the app users and stand out of the crowd. Users are able to find the closest hospital and view the detailed information about the facility to make up their mind to use the hospital's services.

Hospitals are in complete control of the information about their facility by way of an appointed POC who can update the information on Humwell about the Hospital whenever deemed fit. Patient traffic, and sales at hospital's facilities such as pharmacy, emergency room and specialized care units are increased with increased visibility on the digital landscape to the potential end users.
